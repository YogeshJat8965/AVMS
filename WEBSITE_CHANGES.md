## 5. Tab: Services (Comprehensive Detail)

Expanded to match the depth. Each main category should have its own landing page or a detailed drop-down.

### I. Audit & Assurance

- **Statutory Audit:** Compliance with Companies Act 2013 and various statutes
- **Internal & Management Audit:** Operational efficiency, internal control reviews, and risk assessment
- **Bank Audits:** Statutory Branch Audits (SBA), Concurrent Audits, Revenue Audits, and Stock Audits
- **Tax Audit:** Under Section 44AB of the Income Tax Act
- **Forensic Audit:** Fraud investigation, fund-trail analysis, and litigation support
- **Information System (IS) Audit:** Security audits, IT control mapping, and CISA-certified reviews

### II. Taxation (Direct & Indirect)

- **Income Tax:** ITR Filing, Tax Planning for Corporates/Individuals, and TDS/TCS compliance
- **Tax Litigation:** Representation before CIT (Appeals) and Income Tax Appellate Tribunal (ITAT)
- **GST Advisory:** Registration, Monthly/Annual Returns (GSTR-9/9C), and Input Tax Credit (ITC) optimization
- **GST Litigation:** Handling Show Cause Notices (SCN), appeals, and departmental audits
- **International Tax:** NRI Taxation, Double Taxation Avoidance Agreement (DTAA) advisory, and Form 15CA/CB

### III. Corporate & Legal Advisory

- **Business Setup:** Incorporation of Pvt Ltd, LLP, Section 8 (NGO), and OPC
- **ROC Compliance:** Maintaining Statutory Registers, Filing Annual Returns, and Director-related changes
- **Transaction Advisory:** Mergers, Acquisitions, and Business Restructuring
- **Insolvency & Bankruptcy:** Advisory services related to the IBC framework

### IV. Financial & Management Consultancy

- **Project Financing:** Preparation of Project Reports, CMA Data, and Loan Syndication
- **Due Diligence:** Financial and Legal due diligence for investments
- **Startup Services:** Startup India registration, Pitch Decks, and Valuation certificates
- **Outsourced CFO:** Virtual CFO services for SMEs and growing businesses
